Exhibitions

At OPJS School in Raigarh, exhibitions and displays are integral to the educational experience. Students showcase their talents and projects in various subjects, from science to arts. These events encourage creativity, innovation, and practical learning, while also fostering a sense of achievement and community involvement.

Sports

At OPJS School in Raigarh, sports play a vital role in student life. The school offers a wide range of athletic activities, including cricket, football, basketball, and athletics. Emphasizing physical fitness and teamwork, OPJS encourages students to participate in inter-school competitions, fostering a spirit of sportsmanship and excellence.
